How to Submit an Anonymous Tip

Your anonymous tips help us solve and prevent crime in our community. All information provided is kept strictly confidential.

Benefits of submitting a tip:

  • 100% anonymous - your identity is never revealed
  • Information goes directly to investigators
  • You may be eligible for a cash reward

You can submit a tip online through our secure web portal or call our dedicated phone line.
