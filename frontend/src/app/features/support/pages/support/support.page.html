Volunteer Opportunities Join our community and make a difference

One of the primary functions of our team of volunteers is to raise funds & increase awareness of the Crime Stoppers program throughout the Halton region. Our goal is to make volunteering an enjoyable experience within an environment of like-minded community members, where your experience and skills are utilized.

Which Volunteer Positions are Available?

General Volunteer Community Watch Volunteer Board of Directors Volunteer Highschool/University Co-Op Placements

What Do Crime Stoppers Volunteers Do?

Our volunteers can assist at community events, fundraisers, Delta Bingo shifts, awareness campaigns and help us spread the word on this unique organization. We offer resume/interview workshops to assist in your career, share a vast network of contacts to get you in contact with like-minded individuals, a chance to serve your community with pride as we share safety initiatives throughout Halton.

Apply Now

Volunteer Application Volunteer Youth Application Board of Directors Application

Please submit your completed application along with your resume/CV to info@haltoncrimestoppers.ca

Applicants must work or reside in or have a substantive interest in the Halton Region. Our Board of Directors meets every month for approximately 2 hours. All personal information will be kept in the strictest confidence.
