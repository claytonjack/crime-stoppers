Why Join Our Team?
Volunteering with Crime Stoppers Halton offers you the opportunity to make a real impact in your community while developing valuable skills and connections. You'll join a supportive team where your contributions matter, gain meaningful experience, and help create a safer Halton region for everyone.

Note: You must work, reside in, or have a substantial interest in the Halton Region to be considered.

How to Apply
Step 1: Download Form
Select the button below to download the form related to your age group or skill set
Step 2: Complete the Application
Fill out all sections of the application form completely and accurately
Step 3: Submit Your Application
Email your completed application and personal resume to info@haltoncrimestoppers.ca
